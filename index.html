Building in the open

This site is a work in progress. Some things might look a bit raw -- and that's okay! You're very welcome to see this little corner of the Internet evolve in the open while I share a thought or two.

Hi, I am Caro.

I am a designer who codes, a product consultant, and an accessibility advocate.

My expertise spans across design, front-end development, and product strategy, from problem definition to production. I put a special focus on solving problems through collaboration, and I shape my role to be a versatile technologist who connects business, technical, and human aspects to ensure product success.

I seek to understand people and their context through research and testing, design in flows and systems, partner with developers on front-end implementation, facilitate discussions with stakeholders, and leverage data insights to support product strategy and roadmap evolution.

I thrive in cross-functional teams that collaborate and learn in the open.

My design process is defined by my values. I prioritize quality, empathy, and honesty. I make sure that product strategy and business goals are aligned with creating functional, inclusive, and accessible experiences.
